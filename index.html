Welcome to Ottawa's LARGEST 24-hour gaming competition!

From intense gaming, to extraordinary prizes and unbeatable bragging rights, Battle Royale has it all.

Every year, over 100 competitors bring their best to Ottawa's largest 24-hour gaming event, for a chance to win incredible prizes of up to $1000 - and you could be one of them!

If you think you have what it takes to rise to the top or simply want to have a good time, Battle Royale is for you.

Games & Prizes

Every year, Battle Royale has been one of the most epic gaming competitions in Ottawa, with legendary prizes and intense games. And this year is no exception.

Battle Royale XI will feature the five most popular games of 2017, with each game featuring its own tournament and special prizes! Tournaments will take place at different times during the day & night!

This year's prizes will feature awesome stuff from Steam giftcards, to new gaming headphones, and brand new desktops, with some prizes even worth over $1000!!

IMPORTANT: This year, CS:GO will be a major tournament. Tickets for major tournaments will cost $5 more, but prizes will increase substantially in value and the competition and intensity will be more epic.
